# 处置的定义

处置是做出的**决定——**[**不合格**](http://nonconformancereport.org/non-conformity-definition.html)审查过程的结果。  
意思是**“这就是我们将如何处理不符合要求的产品（或服务）”**。

**有四种可能的处理方式：**  

**1\. 放行**——产品将被接受而不采取进一步措施——尽管可能需要做出[让步——规定可以放行的限制。](http://nonconformancereport.org/concession-definition.html)这有时被称为“**按原样使用”**。

**2\. 返工**——对产品采取措施使其符合要求。这可能并不意味着它将与原始合格产品相同。  

**3\. 修理**——对产品采取措施使其符合预期用途。维修后的产品可能仍然不符合要求，这时就需要[**让步。**](http://nonconformancereport.org/concession-definition.html)  

**4.废料**\- 不合格产品不能使用，将被回收或销毁。  

其他常用表达方式如**退货 (RTV)**或**召回**可用于表示任何返工、维修或报废。


# Definition of Disposition

The **disposition** is the decision made - the outcome of the review process of a [**nonconformity**](http://nonconformancereport.org/non-conformity-definition.html).  
Meaning **“This is what we will do with this product (or service) that does not conform with requirements”**.

**There are four possible dispositions:**  
**1\. release** - the product will be accepted without further measures - although a [concession](http://nonconformancereport.org/concession-definition.html) will probably be required - setting out the limits within which it can be released. This is sometimes known as **use-as-is**.  
**2\. rework** - action will be taken on the product to make it conform with the requirements. This might not mean that it will be identical to an original conforming product.  
**3\. repair** - action will be taken on the product to make it acceptable for the intended use. The product might still not conform to requirements after repair, in which case a [**concession**](http://nonconformancereport.org/concession-definition.html) would be required.  
**4\. scrap** - the non-conforming product cannot be used, and will be recycled or destroyed.  
Other common expressions like **Return to Vendor (RTV)** or **recall** can be used to mean any of rework, repair or scrap.
